How long will I have access to the course?

Courses will generally remain available for as long as you keep your LinkedIn account. On rare occasions we may need to remove a course for legal or other reasons. In such rare cases, we will notify you of the change.

If a course isn't what I wanted, can I get a refund?

We apologize if the course didn't meet your expectations. If you are unsatisfied, we offer refunds within 30 days of your purchase. Refunds are not available after 30 days from purchase. Learn about our refund policy.

What else do I get access to when I buy a course?

You will gain access to all supplemental materials of the course, including transcripts, Q&A, and exercise files and quizzes (when applicable).
